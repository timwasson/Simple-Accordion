How to use this little chunk of jQuery.

Doesn't get much easier than this. Just structure your HTML like this:

			<div class="section">
				<h3>Title.</h3>
				<div class="inner">
					<p>sliding content here</p>
				<div>
			</div>

Then just link to the JS file in the footer. Easy peasy.
